Mahatma Gandhi

Salt March led by Mahatma Gandhi on March 12, 1930 when he was 60yrs old.

Here's a time line of Mahatma Gandhi life:

  • Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi was born on 2 October 1869.
  • Gandhi attended University College, London, a constituent college of the University of London.
  • In April 1893, Gandhi aged 23, set sail for South Africa to be the lawyer for Abdullah's cousin.[58][59] He spent 21 years in South Africa, where he developed his political views, ethics and politics.
  • In April 1918, during the latter part of World War I, the Viceroy invited Gandhi to a War Conference in Delhi.
  • At 5:17 pm on 30 January 1948, Gandhi was with his grandnieces in the garden of Birla House (now Gandhi Smriti), on his way to address a prayer meeting, when Nathuram Godse, a Hindu nationalist, fired three bullets into his chest from a pistol at close range.
